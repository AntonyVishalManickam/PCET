Knowledge Hub

Central Library

The PCET Central Library is the heart of our academic community. Spread across a vast area, it houses an extensive collection of books, international journals, research papers, and digital media catering to all branches of engineering and management.

  • Over 50,000 volumes of books
  • Access to IEEE, Springer, and DELNET digital libraries
  • Quiet reading halls and dedicated reference sections
Accommodation

Hostel Facilities

We understand that a comfortable living environment is crucial for academic success. PCET offers separate, secure, and well-furnished hostels for boys and girls within the campus premises.

  • Spacious, well-ventilated rooms
  • Hygienic mess serving nutritious food
  • 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance
Physical Education

Sports & Games

At PCET, we emphasize the physical well-being of our students. Our campus boasts excellent sporting infrastructure to encourage students to participate in various athletic and sports activities.

  • Expansive grounds for Cricket & Football
  • Well-maintained courts for Basketball
  • Indoor sports complex & modern gymnasium
