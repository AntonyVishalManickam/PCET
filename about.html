About PCET

50+ Years of Excellence in Education & Innovation

"To provide an education that works, is what we aim at PCET. We enable our students to sharpen their minds and discover the power behind their hands."

The Park College of Engineering and Technology was started in 1996 and is situated on the National Highway NH 544 in Kaniyur at 28 km east from the industrial city of Coimbatore in Tamilnadu. It is located on a sprawling 30 acres of land surrounded by green fields and coconut Groves on its three sides, and a chain of industries on the other.

The College is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and is approved by AICTE, Delhi. It is also an IS0 Certified Institution. The Director of Technical Education of the Government of Tamilnadu is the supervisory authority over the College.

We focus on providing technical excellence, imbibing the qualities of entrepreneurship & grooming leaders who are ready for the future. As a leading Engineering College in Coimbatore, we diligently monitor global industry advancements, ensuring students receive the best technical education.

Our Vision

"To pioneer potentials of students and faculty in engineering education through world class infrastructure and educational resources for learning, teaching and research."
